Government Staff Package

1. Eligibility

  • The Government Staff Package is available exclusively for employees working in recognized Government Departments (State, Central, or Local bodies).
  • Only staff with valid proof of employment will be considered.

2. Required Documents

To avail this package, staff members must submit the following:

  • Government Department ID Card (valid and updated)
  • Aadhar Card (with complete residential address)
  • Full Name of the Employee
  • Department/Office Name and Address

3. Verification

  • All documents must be original at the time of verification. Photocopies will be collected for record purposes.
  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical reserves the right to confirm details with the concerned department if necessary.

4. Benefits of Government Staff Package

  • Special discounts on eye check-ups, frames, and lenses.
  • Priority services for Government staff.
  • Exclusive optical offers designed for employees and their families (if applicable).

5. Conditions of Use

  • The package is strictly non-transferable and valid only for the Government staff member whose ID is provided.
  • Discounts cannot be clubbed with any other offer or package.
  • Any attempt to misuse this package with false or invalid documents will lead to immediate cancellation of benefits.

6. Liability

  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical is not responsible for errors, omissions, or false declarations made by applicants.
  • The clinic reserves the right to modify, update, or withdraw this package without prior notice.

7. Acknowledgement

  • By availing the Government Staff Package, the employee agrees to follow and accept these terms and conditions.

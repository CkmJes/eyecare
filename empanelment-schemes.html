What Are Empanelment Schemes?

Empanelment schemes are partnerships between healthcare providers and organizations—such as government departments, insurance companies, and corporates—to offer medical services at pre-approved rates or benefits. Through our empanelment programs, eligible individuals can access comprehensive eye checkups, eyewear, and optical services conveniently and at discounted prices.

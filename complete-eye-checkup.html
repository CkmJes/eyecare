Complete Eye Checkup in Chikmagalur

Your Vision Deserves Expert Care

At Glory Eye Clinic & Optical, we believe that healthy vision is the foundation of a confident and productive life. Our Complete Eye Checkup service in Chikmagalur is designed to provide accurate, detailed, and personalized assessments for all age groups — from children to senior citizens.

What Our Complete Eye Checkup Includes

Our advanced computerized eye testing ensures that every aspect of your eye health is examined thoroughly. The checkup includes:

  • Computerized Vision Testing – for precise measurement of eyesight.
  • Refraction Test – to identify refractive errors such as myopia, hyperopia, and astigmatism.
  • Eye Pressure (Glaucoma) Screening – to detect early signs of glaucoma.
  • Retinal & Corneal Examination – to assess internal eye health.
  • Colour Vision & Binocular Vision Tests – to ensure proper eye coordination and colour perception.
  • Digital Report & Expert Consultation – to help you understand your eye condition and recommended solutions.

Types of Contact Lenses We Offer

We offer all major types of lenses to suit your vision and lifestyle needs:

  • Daily Wear Contact Lenses – Perfect for one-day use with ultimate hygiene and convenience.
  • Extended Wear Lenses - Ideal for continuous use over a few days or weeks.
  • Monthly & Bi-weekly Lenses– Cost-effective options with proper care and cleaning.
  • Coloured Contact Lenses – Available in natural and vibrant shades to enhance your look.
  • Toric & Multifocal Lenses – Designed for astigmatism or presbyopia (age- related vision changes).

Disability Package

1. Eligibility

  • The Disability Package is available exclusively for individuals with valid Government-recognized Disability Identification.
  • Only patients with a valid Disability ID Card are eligible.

2. Required Documents

To avail this package, applicants must provide:

  • Disability ID Card (issued by a recognized authority)
  • Aadhar Card (with complete residential address)
  • Full Name of the Individual
  • Office/Institution Name and Address (if applicable)

3. Verification

  • All documents must be original at the time of verification.
  • Photocopies may be collected for clinic records.
  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical reserves the right to confirm the validity of documents with the issuing authority.

4. Benefits of Disability Package

  • Free waiting priority – individuals with disabilities will be given preference and no waiting charges will apply.
  • Special discounts on eye check-ups, frames, and lenses.
  • Supportive care – our clinic ensures maximum assistance for persons with disabilities throughout their visit.
  • Dedicated staff support for mobility and comfort during consultation.

5. Conditions of Use

  • The package is non-transferable and valid only for the individual whose Disability ID is presented.
  • Discounts cannot be combined with other promotional offers or packages.
  • Any attempt to misuse this package with false or invalid documents will result in cancellation of benefits.

6. Liability

  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical will not be held responsible for any false information or misrepresentation provided by applicants.
  • The clinic reserves the right to modify, update, or withdraw this package without prior notice.

7. Acknowledgement

  • By availing the Disability Package, the applicant acknowledges and agrees to abide by these terms and conditions.
  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical is committed to caring most for persons with disabilities and supporting them with dignity and respect.

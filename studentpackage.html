Student Package

1. Eligibility

  • The Student Package is available only for:
    • Primary and High Primary School students
    • PUC students
    • Degree and other college students
  • Students must provide valid proof of identity at the time of availing the package.

2. Required Documents

To be eligible, students must present:

  • School/College ID Card (valid and updated)
  • Aadhar Card (with complete residential address)
  • Full Name of the Student
  • School/College Name and Address

3. Verification

  • All documents must be original and valid. Photocopies will be collected for records.
  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical reserves the right to verify student details with the respective institution if required.

4. Benefits of Student Package

  • Special discounts on eye check-ups and optical products.
  • Affordable frames and lenses designed for students.
  • Exclusive student-only offers may apply from time to time.

5. Conditions of Use

  • The package is non-transferable and can only be used by the student whose name appears on the ID.
  • Discounts cannot be combined with any other promotional offer.
  • Misuse of student identity or submission of false documents will lead to cancellation of benefits.

6. Liability

  • Glory Eye Clinic Optical is not responsible for incorrect or fraudulent information provided by students.
  • The clinic reserves the right to change, modify, or withdraw the package at any time without prior notice.

7. Acknowledgement

  • By availing the Student Package, the student (and parent/guardian if applicable) agrees to abide by these terms and conditions.
